Our expertiese

We bring the latest technologies and a culture of innovation to our work to meet community needs for mobility, connectivity, sustainability and resiliency.

Infrastructure management

Improving the effectiveness and efficiency of infrastructure management is a key challenge. We support our clients by the definition and standardization of there infrastructure management process, from setting goals to determining optimal monitoring and intervention programs, and evaluating the performance of there infrastructure management organisation.

Optimisation of work programs

We help by the determination of optimal intervention strategies for single infrastructure objects and the development of algorithms to generate network intervention plans that maximise net benefit.

Predictive modeling

By integrating data and models, predictions of infrastructure systems can be brought to a new level. Our tools and methods are especially well-suited for predictions of degradation processes and automations of parts of the infrastructure management process.

Risk assessments

Understanding and assessing risks is a key challenge to most of our clients, who often deal with complex systems. We support clients in apprising the risks, quantifying them and setting up efficient management strategies.
