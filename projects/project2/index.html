Asset management for railway infrastructures

We provided recommendations for improved asset management for railway infrastructures

Project overview

Infrastructure managers require indicators to obtain a view of 1) how their asset management process is working, and 2) how much work will be required in the future. These indicators provide infrastructure managers with information as to how they can improve, and ensure that they continue to provide the expected service increasingly efficiently and effectively.
